Overview

The Lamborghini Urus is a luxury super SUV that combines bold design, powerful performance, and exceptional luxury. With cutting-edge technology and Lamborghini’s unmistakable Italian craftsmanship, the Urus redefines the limits of what an SUV can be.

Specifications

Engine: 4.0L V8 Twin-Turbo
Power: 641 hp
Top Speed: 190 mph
0-60 mph: 3.6 seconds
Torque: 627 lb-ft
Drive: All-Wheel Drive

Features

  • Advanced Adaptive Cruise Control
  • Driver Assistance Systems with Lane Departure Warning
  • Customizable Driving Modes (Strada, Sport, Corsa, Terra)
  • Luxury Interior with Premium Leather and Alcantara
  • State-of-the-Art Infotainment System
