Overview

The BMW X5 is a luxury mid-size SUV that combines comfort, performance, and advanced technology. Designed for those who seek adventure without compromising on luxury, the X5 offers the best of BMW's engineering and design.

Specifications

Engine: 3.0L I6 Turbo
Power: 335 hp
Top Speed: 130 mph
0-60 mph: 5.3 seconds
Torque: 330 lb-ft
Drive: All-Wheel Drive
