Overview

The Tesla Model S is a luxury electric sedan that combines high performance with a minimalistic design. Known for its industry-leading range, speed, and innovative technology, the Model S sets the standard for what an electric car can achieve.

Specifications

Range: 396 miles
Top Speed: 200 mph
0-60 mph: 1.99 seconds
Horsepower: 1020 hp
Battery: Long Range
Drive: All-Wheel Drive

Features

  • Autopilot and Full Self-Driving Capability
  • Premium interior with ultra-modern design
  • 17-inch Cinematic Display with 2200 x 1300 resolution
  • Wireless Charging and Custom Audio System
  • Advanced climate control with HEPA filtration
